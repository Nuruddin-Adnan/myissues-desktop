Description

Myissues was established in 2019 with the mission of providing people with access to essential services that enhance their quality of life. Our goal is to deliver support services that are high quality and easily accessible for people who lack these options. We seek to build a community where people feel empowered to thrive and grow in desirable ways.

Your health and wellbeing are our priority. We intend to serve the community and offer promising solutions to gaps in the current services. We have always been receptive to community feedback, and we implement initiatives that meet the greatest needs in the community. We hope to transform the lives of people positively, one service at a time.

Our work supports people across various demographics and ethnic backgrounds. We value inclusivity, and we are open to assisting people from all walks of life. We offer services for homeless people, aging seniors, students, families, and many other groups of people.

We understand that many of these people are vulnerable, and they struggle to get the required support services. They feel like victims of their financial circumstances, and we strive to change these limiting circumstances. Our organization works diligently to ensure that their basic needs will be satisfied with care and comfort.

The team offers food bank services that provide non-perishable food items that supply healthy options to people who struggle with food expenses. We strongly believe that people should not go hungry due to their financial stresses. We care about our community similar to our family, and we believe that no family member should experience extended periods of hunger.

Our services do not stop with the food options. We offer emotional support for seniors and students so that they can navigate life transitions smoothly. Furthermore, we deliver counseling services that are available for tenants to resolve legal disputes.

We created an all-in-one platform that encompasses a range of services that fulfills the needs of various people. Our vision is to create a world where people can overcome their circumstances and live their dreams. No one should be held back by their struggles, and we intend to build a healthy community where everyone is content.
